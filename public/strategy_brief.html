The Flow

01 Webinar Registered

Entry Point: Users sign up via Typeform.

Communication Agenda

Goal: Reduce Absenteeism & Build Anticipation.

Cadence: Alternate days leading up to event + 1 hour before.

Content Focus: Teasers of the 10% concept, success stories (micro-case studies), and logistical reminders to ensure high attendance.

View Welcome Email →

02 Webinar Attended

The Decision Point. The critical filter that splits the funnel three ways.

Communication Agenda

Goal: The Decision Point (Force a Choice).

Cadence: Immediate Post-Webinar.

Content Focus: Urgency (50 seats limit) and clear 3-path options (Commit, Trial, or Opt-out). We eliminate tire kickers here.

View "The Quiet After" Email →

03 Trial User (50k)

Users who booked a trial. Ready to experience.

Communication Agenda

Status: To Be Developed.

Goal: Logistics & Expectation Setting.

Cadence: Alternate Days until Visit.

Content Focus: Practical guides (packing lists, 'What to leave behind'), introductions to the on-ground team, and weather updates for the specific collective to ensure a seamless 'wow' experience.

04 Active Prospect

Post-Trial Users evaluating full commitment.

Communication Agenda

Status: To Be Developed.

Goal: Reflection & Conversion.

Cadence: Day 1, Day 3, Day 7 Post-Visit.

Content Focus: "How does the city feel now?" (Contrast), detailed breakdown of membership math, and invitations to a final closing call with the founder.

05 Member Onboarding

Committed members.

Communication Agenda

Status: To Be Developed.

Goal: Welcome & Orientation.

Cadence: Weekly for First Month.

Content Focus: Access to community portal, introductions to other founding members, scheduling the first official stay, and payment finalization.

03. The Three Actions

Every communication piece drives the user toward one of three specific actions, managed automatically via n8n webhooks:

1. The Pause (Opt-Out)

Action: User clicks "Opt-out for now".

Result: Deal marked as LOST in Pipedrive.

→ Test Opt-Out Flow

2. The Trial (Book Stay)

Action: User clicks "Book Trial Stay".

Result: Deal moved to Trial User stage.

→ Test Trial Flow

3. The Unlock (Full Sub)

Action: User clicks "Subscribe Now".

Result: Deal moved to Member Onboarding stage.

→ Test Subscription Flow
