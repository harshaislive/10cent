## Question 1: Digital Disconnection
**Question**: When did you last disconnect for 24 hours?

**Answer Options**:
- "I can't remember"
- "Last week"
- "A month ago"
- "I often disconnect"
- "Most weekends"

**Insights**:
- For "I can't remember": "The machine never stops unless you stop it. 30 nights isn't a vacation; it's a retrieval of your own mind."
- For recent answers: "You know the feeling of the off-switch. The trick is keeping it off long enough to hear yourself think. Try 10%."
- For regular answers: "You are already fighting the good fight. Now, give your resolve a headquarters."

- "City noises have taken over"

## Question 4: Seasonal Awareness
**Question**: Do you experience seasons as calendar dates or as actual changes in your environment?

**Answer Options**:
- "I've lost track of seasons"
- "Seasons change outside my office window"
- "I notice but don't experience them"
- "I try to get out on weekends"
- "I live by the seasons"

**Insights**:
- For lost track/office answers: "We have paved over the calendar. Reconnect with the rhythm of the earth, not the fiscal quarter."
- For notice but don't experience answers: "Looking at rain through glass isn't the same as feeling it. Break the glass. Step out for 10% of your year."
- For weekend/living by seasons answers: "You are rare. Most people live in air-conditioning. You live in the world. You belong here."

## Question 5: Starry Skies
**Question**: When was the last time you saw a sky full of stars, unpolluted by city lights?

**Answer Options**:
- "Can't see them from the city"
- "I remember seeing them as a kid"
- "Occasionally on clear nights"
- "When I travel away from cities"
- "I often look for them"

**Insights**:
- For city/childhood memory answers: "The stars didn't go away. You did. 30 nights a year brings the universe back into view."
- For occasional/travel answers: "A glimpse is nice. A gaze changes your perspective. Don't settle for glimpses."
- For regular/often answers: "Then you know how small our problems look from down here. Keep looking up."

## Purpose of These Questions

These reflection questions were designed to:

1. **Create Self-Awareness**: Help visitors recognize their disconnection from nature
2. **Establish Need**: Highlight the gap between their current state and natural living
3. **Offer Solution**: Position "30 nights a year" as the answer to their nature deficit
4. **Qualify Leads**: Identify visitors who are ready for a wilderness-based solution

Each question follows a pattern where users select their current relationship with nature, and receive personalized insights that lead toward the same conclusion: 30 nights in nature annually (10% of the year) can restore their connection to the natural world.
